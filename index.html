Welcome to DogFacts.com

Before purchasing a new puppy or Dog there are several things that need to be considered and without making these considerations can result in future issues for you and your new fuzzy friend. In the worst case scenario you could wind up having to give your new family member to a shelter or having to put them down if you didn't make those considerations before getting them. Here at DogFacts we have helpful information you need to get started on deciding which dog or pup fits best into your family so that both you don't wind up in that situation down the road and your new furry family member gets the best chance at living a long happy life.
